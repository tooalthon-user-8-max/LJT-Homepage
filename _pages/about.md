# Junteng Liu

First-year PhD candidate at [HKUST NLP Group](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~jhe/), advised by Professor [Junxian He](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~jhe/). My research focuses on **Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning**, with specific interests in:
- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability
